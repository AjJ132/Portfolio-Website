ProServ

ProServ is a platform that enhances the relationship between coaches and their athletes by facilitating the creation and distribution of workouts.

Constructed with Microsoft's Blazor and ASP.NET, ProServ is a robust, feature-rich platform that offers secure logins, facilitates payments, and even includes a mobile ios app.

Currently live and fully maintained, ProServ hosted its first customer in 2023. The initial product development took approximately three months. ProServ's technical infrastructure is powered by Azure Web App hosting, SQL technologies, and maintains a CI/CD pipeline through Github Actions.
